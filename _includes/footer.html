About Java Square

Java Square, nestled in the heart of historic downtown Opelousas, is more than just a cafe. It's a blend of rich history and modern coffee culture, housed in the iconic 1927 Parish & Trust Bank building. Venture inside and discover the old bank vault, now transformed into an intimate seating nook for our guests.
