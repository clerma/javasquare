Vault Readings

October 23, 2018 Carlos Lerma

Java Square now offers weekly storytimes for children. Java's vault readings help foster early literacy skills needed for children to be school-ready. Our storytimes for babies, toddlers, and preschoolers focus on developing early literacy skills—those skills necessary to begin to learn to read. We have selected stories to reach and teach a multi-age audience from 3 to 7-year-olds so come out and join the fun!
