§ 01 — The Room

An old bank,
re-deposited
as a living room.

The building at 103 West Landry held the town's savings for most of a century. We kept the marble, the tall ceilings, and the vault door — then added cream walls, navy cabinetry, and a bar where the teller line used to be. Sit in the vault with a cappuccino and consider it a small act of civic reclamation.
